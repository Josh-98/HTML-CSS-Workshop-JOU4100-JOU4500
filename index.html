Hello!

Hi and welcome to my page! My name is Joshua Ambar and I am very happy you are here. Throughout your time on this website you will learn all there is to no about me. On my site, you will learn about my life, my goals and my hobbies. Stick around and enjoy!

Just a little bit of school...

When high school was coming to a close, I never really had a thought of what I wanted to do with my life so I decided to jump in a history degree at Carleton University which lasted all but two weeks. I took time off to realize I wanted to go to Algonquin College for Pre-Animation. It was a year program and I really grew my drawing skills however I did not get into Animation because my layout art was not up to standard. I looked to my hobbies to see what I wanted to do for a career and comic books came to mind. I saw that a comic book editor position requirement was a journalism degree and that made up my mind. And as you can tell by the links above, I have been quite successful with my articles!

What does "Hello, World!" mean? Here's Wikipedia's answer:
